Comprehensive Travel Insurance
Combines medical coverage with trip protection benefits: trip cancellation / interruption, lost baggage, flight delays, and emergency evacuation. Best suited for B-1/B-2 visitors with pre-paid non-refundable trips, business travelers, or those wanting all-in-one coverage. Note: medical benefit limits in travel insurance are typically lower than dedicated visitor health plans — review the medical sub-limits carefully.

Indian Insurance Plans — Buy Before You Leave
Indian insurance companies offer outbound international travel policies that cover your US visit. These must typically be purchased before departure from India. They are denominated in INR, paid through Indian bank accounts, and claims are processed by the Indian insurer (sometimes through a US assistance company). A good option if you want to manage everything from India, or if your US visa has been approved but you haven't left yet.

Heads up: Indian travel insurance plan terms, premiums, and pre-existing condition rules change frequently. Always verify directly from the insurer's website before purchase. Medical benefit limits for US coverage can be significantly lower than US-based visitor health plans (Sections 1 & 2). For senior travelers (60+) with pre-existing conditions, Section 1 plans generally offer superior US hospital coverage.
